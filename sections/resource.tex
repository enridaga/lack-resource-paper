The LACK knowledge graph is published as Linked Open Data under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) licence. Version 1.1 was released in April 2026 and is accessible through multiple modalities at \url{https://climatesense-project.eu/lack/}: as a downloadable Turtle dump (\texttt{KG.ttl}), as a live SPARQL endpoint powered by QLever\footnote{\url{https://sparql.climatesense.kmi.tools/climatesense}}, and through an interactive exploration interface. The ontology is separately available in Turtle and OWL Manchester Syntax serialisations, identified by the persistent namespace URI \url{https://purl.net/climatesense/lack/ns#}. Table~\ref{tab:kg-stats} summarises the key statistics of the resource; a full breakdown of relation counts by property is available on the LACK website.

The LACK ontology has been submitted for registration in LOV~\cite{lov}; % TODO: update with confirmed LOV URI once registration is complete
the dataset is archived on Zenodo at \url{https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19846035}, and a VoID/DCAT description is provided as \texttt{lack-dataset.ttl}, covering distributions, the SPARQL endpoint, and linksets to Wikidata and DBpedia. The resource is maintained by KMi as part of its research line on climate disinformation, updated yearly, and guaranteed available for a minimum of five years under funder open data policy.
