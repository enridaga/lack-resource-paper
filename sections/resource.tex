The LACK knowledge graph is published as Linked Open Data under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) licence. Version 1.0 was released in April 2026 and is accessible through multiple modalities at \url{https://climatesense-project.eu/lack/}: as a downloadable Turtle dump (\texttt{KG.ttl}), as a live SPARQL endpoint powered by QLever\footnote{\url{https://sparql.climatesense.kmi.tools/climatesense}}, and through an interactive exploration interface. The ontology is separately available in Turtle and OWL Manchester Syntax serialisations, identified by the persistent namespace URI \url{https://purl.net/climatesense/lack/ns#}. Table~\ref{tab:kg-stats} summarises the key statistics of the resource; a full breakdown of relation counts by property is available on the LACK website.
